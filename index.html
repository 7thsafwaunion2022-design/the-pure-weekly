About The Pure Weekly

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind this academic publication platform.

Our Mission

The Pure Weekly is an academic publication platform established by the 7th Batch of Noorul Hidaya Islamic Academy. Our mission is to provide a digital space for students to showcase their literary talents, share academic insights, and engage in intellectual discourse. We believe in nurturing creativity and academic excellence through publication opportunities.

Platform Features

  • Automatic view counting for each publication
  • Days-ago calculation showing publication age
  • Category-based organization (Poems, Stories, Essays, Letters)
  • Regular literary events and competitions
  • Academic integrity and quality review process

Design & Development

This platform was designed and developed by the 7th Batch of Noorul Hidaya Islamic Academy as an academic project. The design focuses on user experience, readability, and showcasing literary works in their best light. The automatic counting systems are implemented to provide authors with immediate feedback on their work's reception.
